Our Product

Data breaches, the misuse of our online information and the compromising of our accounts, is an ever growing issue in todays digital landscape. Our survey shows that the general user is interested in the theme but struggling to get relevant and precise information on the issue at hand.

How can we as a society take steps towards regaining control of our privacy if we do not understand or struggle to find security information, for ourselves and the sites we visit?


This is what we want to change with WARD!

  • WARD will provide personalized updates if any of your accounts might be compromised
  • Alerts if any of the sites you use have been breached
  • Simplify user agreements on services you use
  • We protect your privacy on the web

DID YOU KNOW THAT?

The first six months of 2019 there were 3800 publicly breaches exposing 4.1 billion records, according to Risk Based Security Research (forbes).

Over 10 % of the Norwegian population have been affected. Primarily, due to data breaches in services like Dropbox and Linkedin. The consequences are not immediately visible (e24).

OUR SURVEY SHOWS..

According to our survey, internet users (76 %) actually don´t know whether their mail or password have been sold or shared on the web.

At the same time, the survey shows that over 50 % want help dealing with these issues.

WARD is here for you.

Breach update

WARD will give you the personal data breach updates you need to get in charge and have the overview.
When you log in to websites WARD automatically shows whether your user have been breached.

Relevant breaches

For every site you visit, WARD will show you all previous breaches and give a risk rating.
WARD keep your browsing life easy and safe with breach logging for every site WARD tracks.

User licence and agreement tool

Probably, you´ve neither understood nor read the user licence and agreement for most of the services you´re using on daily basis.
WARD helps you cut the crap and gives you the essence of the agreement between you and the service provider. To preserve your privacy, WARD will show you the most relevant and important points in the agreement.
If they update, we´ll let you know.
